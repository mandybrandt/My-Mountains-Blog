The Mountain Called "Life"

I love mountain adventures: hiking, skiing, snow-shoeing, mountain-climbing, anything that involves being in the mountains! I love mountain adventures because they teach me about life. When I climb a mountain, I learn that I can do hard things. I can set a goal and accomplish it. At the top of the mountain the views are amazing and inspiring. At the top of a mountain I feel free... free to make my life whatever I want it to be; and I know I can do it! At times, the mountain called Life will feel like a steep climb. Sometimes there will be boulders and raging rivers in my way. Sometimes, I will slip and fall and have to pull myself back up. Sometimes the trail is difficult to find. Yet, I know that as I continue to climb the mountain of life, as I overcome each obstacle, I become stronger and more confident in my ability to reach the top.

My Mountains

Each mountain has a different story to tell; and each mountain has a new lesson to be learned. While climbing a particularly difficult mountain, my son once made a comment about how cool it would be if there were roads to take you to the top of each mountain. As we pondered that idea, we both agreed that the more difficult the journey, the more spectacular the views. We then compared the trail to our psiritual journey through life. We decided that the parking lot represents the telestial world. Our surroundings are still beautiful, but if we climb a little higher, it gets so much better. The journey on the trail is like the terrestrial kingdom. The journey is difficult at times, but along the way there are moments of peace along with some amazing views. Every now and then, we get glimpses of our destination, the peak of the mountain. It beckons to us and we are motivated by what we know we will find there. Every time we reach a summit, we feel that we have conquered the world. We have made it to the top. We are above every ache and pain and sorrow. We experience pure joy as we stand in wonder and awe at what we can see. Our perspective changes as our view opens up to endless sky and endless peaks. And, every peak is different; just as in the Kingdom of Heaven there are many mansions. Join me on each of my journeys to some of the highest peaks in the West, where each trail has its own story to tell, and each peak is a unique paradise.
